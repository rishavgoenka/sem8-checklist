Why this order? Unit 1 builds the vocabulary (threat, vulnerability, risk, attack types, defenses) that every later unit assumes you already know. Forensics (Unit 2) comes next because "how evidence is gathered" underpins the legal discussion in Unit 5. Mobile security (Unit 3) is grouped with forensics since both are highly practical, example-driven units. Organizational implications (Unit 4) and Law (Unit 5) come last — they build on the technical foundation to discuss cost, ethics, and legal consequences.
